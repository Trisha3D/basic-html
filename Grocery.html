Grocery Shopping:

Grocery shopping is a regular activity that involves selecting and purchasing food and household items, often guided by a list or budget to meet daily or weekly needs. It can be a practical way to plan meals, save money by choosing seasonal or discounted products, and explore new ingredients for cooking. Whether done at a supermarket, local market, or online, it plays a key role in maintaining a healthy and organized lifestyle.
