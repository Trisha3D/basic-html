Cooking:

Cooking is the art and science of preparing food by combining ingredients and applying heat or other techniques to create meals that nourish and delight. It can be a creative outlet, a way to express care for others, and a practical skill that promotes healthier eating and self-sufficiency. Whether you're following a recipe or improvising with what's on hand, cooking brings together flavors, cultures, and traditions in a deeply personal and often joyful experience.
